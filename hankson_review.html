Section 01

Used To

Past habits that are no longer true. Watch out for the "greedy verb" trap and the is used to mix-up.

1
The Three Forms
過去習慣 — no longer true now
Positive She used to have short hair.  （以前有短髮）
Negative She didn't use to eat vegetables. ← no "d" on use!
Question Did she use to live here? ← no "d" on use!
💡 The Greedy Verb Rule: When did or didn't appears, it steals the past tense — drop the "d" from "used" → write use to.
⚠️ The Big Trap
used to + verb
used to
過去習慣（已停止）
a past habit — finished
I used to wake up at 6am.
(I don't anymore.)
is/am/are used to + V-ing
is used to
習慣於（現在）
accustomed to — right now
Tom is used to waking up early.
(It's his routine now.)
✗ Tom used to playing video games every night. It's his routine now.
✓ Tom is used to playing video games every night.
現在的習慣 → is used to + V-ing
Q1Multiple Choice

Anne _____ have short hair, but now she has long hair. She is really beautiful.

Q2Multiple Choice

Harry: _____ Alice use to be a teacher?
William: Yes, she taught at a junior high school from 1990 to 2002.

Q3Fill in the Blank

Q4Fill in the Blank

_____ she use to live in Taipei? Yes, she did.

Q5Fill in the Blank

She _____ eat vegetables, but now she loves broccoli.
Hint: negative form

Q6Multiple Choice · Trap!

Tom _____ playing video games every night. It's his daily routine now.

Section 02

Comparatives & Superlatives

Comparing two things vs. the best/worst of a group. Watch for "than" and "the".

2
The Three Patterns
比較級 / 最高級 / as…as
Comparative 比較級
-er / more … than
Comparing 2 things
Janet is taller than Linda.
This is more useful than that.
Superlative 最高級
the -est / the most …
Best/worst in a group
Karen is the most interesting of all.
He is the tallest in the class.
As … as Ted is as healthy as Daniel.  （一樣健康）equal comparison
⚡
The "than" rule: See than in the sentence? → Must use comparative. the most never goes with than.
The "of all" rule: See of all? → Must use superlative.
✗ No other machine is the most expensive than it.
✓ No other machine is more expensive than it.
見到 "than" → 用比較級，不用最高級

Q4Fill in the Blank

Q5Fill in the Blank

(young → superlative)

Q6Multiple Choice

Section 03

Vocabulary

Key words from Unit 1–2. Read the context carefully before choosing.

3
Words to Know
Context clues — read the whole sentence first
actuallyActually — introduces a surprising or extra fact.  （其實 / 事實上）
tipTip — helpful advice or a trick to solve a problem
fitting roomFitting room — where you try on clothes in a store  （試衣間）
agreeAgree — to have the same opinion  （同意）
usefulUseful — helpful, convenient  （有用的）
💡 Strategy: Don't just pick the first word that looks right — check that it fits the whole sentence, especially the second half.
