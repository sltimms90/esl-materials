1
Myself  vs.  By Myself
我自己做 vs. 一個人做
Myself
myself
我自己做（強調親自）
I did it — nobody else!
I cooked dinner myself.
(My parents didn't cook it.)
By Myself
by myself
我一個人（強調獨自）
I was alone!
I went to the park by myself.
(No friends came with me.)
💡 Quick test: Ask yourself — am I saying WHO did it (myself) or ALONE (by myself)?
✏️  Quick Check — Rule 1

Find the ONE mistake and fix it.

Q1
I don't need help with my homework. I can do it by myself.
✓ This sentence is already CORRECT! 「by myself」 = without help = alone. That fits perfectly here.

2
最高級 + 所有格：Drop "the"!
My / His / Her / Tom's + 最高級 → 不用 the
🔑 The word my / his / her / Tom's already replaces the word the. You can't use both!
✗ He is my the best friend.
✓ He is my best friend.
"my" = 所有格，已經替代了 "the"
✗ Summer is the shop's the busiest season.
✓ Summer is the shop's busiest season.
"the shop's" = 所有格，已經替代了 "the"
✏️  Quick Check — Rule 2

Find the ONE mistake and fix it.

Q2
Anna is my the smartest sister.
✗ Remove "the"! → Anna is my smartest sister.  （my 已代替 the）
3
"In" vs. "Of" with 最高級
在哪個範圍裡？地方用 in，數量用 of
Use IN 用 in
📍 A place or group
地方 / 群體
She is the tallest girl in my class.
He is the best player in the team.
Use OF 用 of
🔢 A number or "all"
數字 / all
She ate the most of the five kids.
Of all the boys, he runs fastest.
⚡ Test Trap: When you see a number (five, three, ten...) or the word all, always use of!
✗ In the five snacks, cake is the yummiest.
✓ Of the five snacks, cake is the yummiest.
✏️  Quick Check — Rule 3

Find the ONE mistake and fix it.

Q3
In the four seasons, winter is the coldest.
✗ Change "In" → "Of"! → Of the four seasons, winter is the coldest.  （four = 數字，用 of）
4
比較級 = 最高級？Any other / All the other
用比較級句型來表達「最…」的意思
💡 這兩個句型意思相同！比較級 + than any other 或 than all the other = 最高級！
Formula 公式 After "than" 後面接 Meaning 意思
比較級 + than any other
any other + 單數名詞
singular noun (一個)
 = 最高級
比其他任何一個都…
比較級 + than all the other
all the other + 複數名詞
plural noun (很多個)
 = 最高級
比其他所有的都…
✓ Ellen is shorter than any other girl in her class.
= Ellen is the shortest girl in her class.  （girl = 單數）
✓ Chris is thinner than all the other kids in the hospital.
= Chris is the thinnest kid in the hospital.  （kids = 複數）
⚠️ SAME GROUP vs. DIFFERENT GROUP
同一個群體 → 加 other
玉山 is in Taiwan,
comparing to other Taiwan mountains
Yushan is taller than any other mountain in Taiwan.
不同群體 → 不加 other
玉山 is in Taiwan,
comparing to Japan mountains
Yushan is taller than any mountain in Japan.
✏️  Quick Check — Rule 4

Choose the correct answer.

Q4a
George is stronger than any other ______ on the team.
(A) men    (B) man
✓ Answer: (B) man any other → 後面接單數名詞
Q4b
比較級改寫成最高級：
Josh is quieter than any other student in his class.
Josh is the quietest student in his class. 比較級 + than any other + 單數名詞 → the + 最高級 + 名詞
Q4c
最高級改寫成比較級：
This robot is the most special toy in the store.
This robot is more special than all the other toys in the store. the + 最高級 + 複數名詞 → 比較級 + than all the other + 複數名詞
⚡ EXAM CHEAT CODES
見到 my/his/her/Tom's + 最高級 → 不用 the
見到 數字 / all + 最高級 → 用 of
見到 地方 / 群體 + 最高級 → 用 in
any other → 後面接 單數名詞
all the other → 後面接 複數名詞
同群體比較 → 加 other；不同群體 → 不加 other
